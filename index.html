About Naturals Academy

Our Vision

The Kottayam branches of Naturals Academy were founded with one clear goal—to empower creative minds with the right tools, techniques, and confidence to pursue successful careers in the beauty industry. Since our inception, we've trained hundreds of students who now thrive as makeup artists, hair stylists, skincare professionals, and beauty entrepreneurs across India and abroad.

Our Mission

We believe in hands-on, experiential learning where every student is encouraged to create, experiment, and innovate. Our aim is not just to train, but to inspire.

Where Passion Meets Profession

At Naturals Academy Kottayam, we,re passionate about turning creativity into a rewarding career. We offer advanced courses in Makeup, Hair, Skin, Cosmetology, and Beauty Therapy, designed to meet global industry standards. With expert faculty, hand-on training, and strong placemnet support, our mission is to nurture the next generation of beauty professionals and entrepreneurs. Whether you,re a student, working professional, or career switcher, Naturals Academy Kottayam helps you unloack your true potential and build a future full of opportunities in the glamorous world of beauty and fashion.
